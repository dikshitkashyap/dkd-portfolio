BlackBuck Pay — UPI on PPI

A complete UPI product built on a closed-loop PPI wallet

Designed the full UPI experience — send money, pay merchants, scan QR, recurring mandates, transaction history — on top of a closed-loop PPI wallet. One designer. Every edge case. Every failure state.

KEY DECISIONS
01Showed only UPI balance — not all wallet balances — to prevent unanswerable questions
02Built a transaction state taxonomy from systematic competitive analysis across GPay, PhonePe, Paytm
03Designed merchant autopay as a trust problem — 8+ distinct failure states, each with a clear next action

BBPS FASTag Recharges

Bringing fragmented recharge behaviour back into the ecosystem

35% of active FASTag users were recharging via third-party apps. Designed and led regulatory approval for a BBPS-powered recharge experience that brought them back.

KEY DECISIONS
01Anchored on super-app patterns (GPay/PhonePe), not trucking app conventions
02Challenged standard BBPS biller selection — negotiated VRN auto-fetch with NPCI
03V1 failed at 62% task success; V2 redesign crossed 90% after structural rethink
